Ghost Ball Method
Pool Practice Trainer

The ghost ball method is the most effective aiming system in pool. It teaches you to visualise an imaginary ball at the exact contact point — then send your cue ball there perfectly.

1
Place the object ball anywhere on the table by clicking
2
Choose a target pocket from the panel on the right
3
The ghost ball appears — the exact spot your cue ball must reach
4
Place your cue ball, aim at the ghost ball, and shoot
5
After a miss, drag the ball to try from a new position
💡 Pro tip: Move your mouse further from the cue ball for finer angle control. The angle display shows your precision in real time.

Free interactive ghost ball method practice tool for pool, snooker and billiards players. Learn how to aim using the ghost ball technique. Improve your potting accuracy with this free billiards aiming trainer suitable for beginners and advanced players.
